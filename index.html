Authentic Jamaican Baking

A local stop for patties, bread, and hot plates.

Allan's is known for tender, flaky patties with generous fillings, fresh hardo bread, coco bread, and familiar Jamaican sweets.

Most visits are quick, parking is available in the plaza lot, and frozen patties are available by the dozen for home.
